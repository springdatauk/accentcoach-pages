Common Questions

Why does the app ask for microphone access?
The microphone is used only when you record a practice phrase for pronunciation feedback.

Why does the app ask for speech recognition access?
The app uses Apple Speech recognition to transcribe the phrase you read aloud.

Why does the app ask for camera access?
The camera is used only when you choose Scan Text in Reading Lab to capture printed text for practice.

How do I manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID subscription settings.
